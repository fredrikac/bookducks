Welcome to BookDucks!

BookDucks is an online book swapping community. We have a wide variety of analogue books (you know the ones printed on actual paper) and audio books (on CD). Add your own books and choose from our digital library!

How it works

  1. Create your profile
  2. Upload the books you want to share with the community
  3. Send an email to the owner of the book you're interested in
  4. Sender pays for postage fees
  5. Be nice - this community is built on trust
